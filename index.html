Making your tv, film or podcast sound professional


I bring an experienced ear to the projects I work on, plus an approach rooted in sound theory and academic understanding.
I am involved in audio production and post-production professionally and academically, working on feature films as dialogue editor, composer and re-recording mixer; and having lectured in music production, sound design and post-production sound.
I love to work with sound and can't wait to hear about your project- get in touch for a chat, or send me a question.

Dialogue Edit

This often un-mentioned yet vital part of the post-production process is the first step in a sound edit.
Through microphone choices, editing and sound shaping to adding ambience and room tone, I can create something clean, watchable, and ready for the addition of sound design and music.

Sound Design

The creative elements of a soundtrack can add so much to the ambience of a project. Correct sound design is vital to tell the story; if you get it right, it will be as important as picture or dialogue.
I can add sound effects or create Foley sounds if and where required.

Re-Recording Mixer

Often also referred to as a Dubbing Mixer, this is where all the sound elements are balanced, panned and effected to create the overall final audio mix.
You may need stereo or surround sound mixes; get in touch to discuss your specific requirements.
