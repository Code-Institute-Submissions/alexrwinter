Sound Design

The creative elements of a soundtrack can add so much to the ambience of a project. Correct sound design is vital to tell the story; if you get it right, it will be as important as picture or dialogue.
I can add sound effects or create Foley sounds if and where required.

Re-Recording Mixer

Often also referred to as a Dubbing Mixer, this is where all the sound elements are balanced, panned and effected to create the overall final audio mix.
You may need stereo or surround sound mixes; get in touch to discuss your specific requirements.
